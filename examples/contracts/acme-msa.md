# Master Services Agreement

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between
**Northwind Studio, LLC**, a Delaware limited liability company ("Provider"),
and **Acme Incorporated**, a New York corporation ("Client").

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026. This Agreement continues through December 31, 2026.

## 1. Services

Provider will deliver consulting services as described in each Statement of Work.

## 2. Fees

Client shall pay Provider a monthly retainer of **$8,000 per month**, invoiced
on the first business day of each month.

## 3. Expenses and Subcontractors

Provider may pass through pre-approved software and hosting expenses with
receipts. Subcontractor costs are reimbursable up to **$5,000 per month**.

## 4. Payment Terms

All invoices are due **Net 30** from the invoice date.
